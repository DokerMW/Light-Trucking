ABOUT US

We are a busy freight transportation company which arranges and completes hundreds of shipments each week. We work with a wide variety of business types and our customers are across all the US. Our terminal is located in New Jersey.

Light Trucking Inc is evolving and growing fast. That is why we have a huge demand for truck Owner Operators! Join our team and we will individually discuss your driving destinations and work conditions! We do not send our drivers to drive in snow or too crowded cities like NY. With us, you can enjoy working in the US in comfortable conditions. Truck drivers are welcomed too!

We expect to find friendly and positive members who can complete our team. Light Trucking Inc is respectful towards everyone and always strives to do what it takes to make our drivers happy and satisfied.
